Abstract

Our study explores how AI systems communicate uncertainty through visualization. As artificial intelligence increasingly informs high-stakes decision-making, understanding the reliability of these models becomes critical. We look at how different studies say visualization affects user trust. By synthesizing current frameworks, this review highlights effective design patterns that align human perception with algorithmic probabilistic outputs, thereby fostering calibrated trust in intelligent user interfaces.
